Manifesto

Intro

We are imaginers and strategists, driven by planetary and post-biological causes. We not only conceive and design products, systems, civilizations, and worlds, but we also create art and harmless vandalism. We are something between a speculative design studio, a societal laboratory, and a distributed intelligence collective. We don't fit easily into a box, but we function as catalysts where there is purpose, chaos, and vision.

Purpose

To achieve large-scale human coordination to transform humanity's existential risks into the greatest opportunity to reinvent ourselves as a civilization.

Mission

To empower impact organizations and their interoperability.

Vision

To be a global catalyst in the transition towards post-biological life, serving as the consultant for leading impact organizations.
